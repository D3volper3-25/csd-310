Parenting Corner

Me:How do you balance your demanding job with parenting?

Mark: It’s not easy, but I make it work. I’m lucky to have a supportive family who helps when on the road. And when I’m home, Liam and I have our little routines. We build forts, read bedtime stories, and go on mini adventures in our backyard. He’s my co-pilot in life!
